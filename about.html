Who We Are

A Passion for Events

Rhodsbeth Events was born in 2021 out of a passion for helping our clients mold their dreams into reality. Since our inception, we’ve committed ourselves to creating impactful and creative experiences for our clients and their guests, no matter the size of the event.

Our team strives to meet and exceed the needs of everyone involved. This is because we understand the importance of working with (not just for) our clients in order to make their events unforgettable, forging memories that last long after the last guest leaves.

Vision

  • To create a memorable experience that leaves a lasting impression on our clients and their guests by creating one-of-a-kind events that inspire, delight, and create a unique atmosphere that reflects the client's brand and values.

    • Mission

  • To preovid exceptional service and attention to detail at every stage of the event planning process, from conception to execution.
  • To bring our clients' vision to life while staying on budget and delivering events that exceed their expectations and build lasting relationships with our clients and vendors, and to continuously improve our services to meet the ever-evolving needs of the event planning industry.
